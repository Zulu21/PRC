What is PRC (Paparts Running Community)?

Paparts Running Community is a vibrant, inclusive group of jogging enthusiasts who come together to share their passion for jogging, support each other's goals, and promote health and wellness in our community. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small group of friends to a community of over 500 active members.

We welcome joggers of all levels - from beginners taking their first steps to seasoned runners. Our community jogs together, trains together, and celebrates every milestone together. Whether you're looking to improve your endurance, enjoy social jogging, or simply reap the mental and physical benefits of jogging with like-minded people, PRC is the place for you.

Every Saturday morning, you'll find our group jogging together through scenic routes, chatting, and building friendships that extend beyond the track. We're more than just a jogging club - we're a family that supports each other in fitness and in life.
